**What improved:**
- **Removed the "Show Answer" button** — you can't cheat; a hint reveals only the *next* letter, which you still have to place yourself.
- **Romanization on every consonant card** — each Thai letter now shows its sound underneath.
- **Progressive 5-level system** — instead of all 44 at once, the exercise unlocks in stages (5 → 5 → 10 → 10 → all 44), so it builds confidence rather than overwhelming.

**Why it's better:** manageable chunks, no shortcut to cheat with, and the romanization reinforces the sound while you learn the order.
