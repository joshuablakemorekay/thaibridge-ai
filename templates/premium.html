🪷 Why some things are free

I value generosity (dāna). The Buddha's teachings were freely given, so the Theravada Dhamma teachings and the meditation timer & techniques are free for everyone — no account, no subscription, no level required.

The AI features are different: every AI message costs real money to run. So the AI tutoring tools — including the Culture & Dhamma Q&A — are a Pro product. The teachings are always accessible; the AI assistant is the part you pay for, and it helps keep the whole project running.

🪷 Our Mission

ThaiBridge AI is designed to help foreign monks and expats learn Thai effectively — blending language, culture and Dhamma so the words you learn carry real meaning.
