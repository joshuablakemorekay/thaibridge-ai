🌱 Practice Guidance

For Beginners:

  • Start with 10-15 minutes of Anapanasati (breath meditation)
  • Focus on establishing stable attention before attempting insight practices
  • Consistency matters more than duration - daily practice is key

For Intermediate Practitioners:

  • Extend sessions to 30-45 minutes
  • Begin with samatha to calm the mind, then shift to vipassana
  • Practice Metta (loving-kindness) to overcome ill-will

For Advanced Practitioners:

  • Attend meditation retreats for intensive practice
  • Work with a qualified teacher for personalized guidance
  • Study the Satipatthana Sutta in depth
