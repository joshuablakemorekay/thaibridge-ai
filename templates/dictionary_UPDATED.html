🎯 Why Paiboon?

The Paiboon romanization system is widely considered the best system for English speakers learning Thai because:

  • Consistent tone marking - Every syllable shows its tone
  • Intuitive for English speakers - Uses familiar letter combinations
  • Distinguishes similar sounds - Separates 'dt' from 'd' and 't'
  • Shows vowel length - Doubled vowels indicate long sounds
  • Widely adopted - Used in many learning materials and apps
