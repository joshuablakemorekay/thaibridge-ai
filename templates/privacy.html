🔒 Privacy ความเป็นส่วนตัว

ThaiBridge AI is a learning project that currently runs locally. This page is an honest summary of how it handles data — not a formal legal policy.

  • Accounts: if you sign up, the app stores your username, email, and a securely hashed password (never the password itself).
  • Learning progress: your level, XP and unlocked sections are kept in your browser session.
  • AI tutor: messages you send to the AI tutor are processed by the Anthropic Claude API to generate replies.
  • No tracking or ads: the app does not use advertising or third-party tracking.

A full privacy policy will be written before the app is deployed for real users.
