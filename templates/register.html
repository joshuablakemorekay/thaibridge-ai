Thai has 9 distinct levels of formality, from sacred monastic language to vulgar slang. Understanding these registers is crucial for appropriate communication and shows cultural awareness.

🎯 The 9-Level System

Level 4: Literary 🎭  |  Level 5: Technical 🔬  |  Level 6: Formal 🤵
Level 7: Neutral 💬  |  Level 8: Casual 😊  |  Level 9: Vulgar 🚫

💡 Key Takeaways

  • As a foreigner, your default should be Level 7 (Neutral) with ครับ/ค่ะ particles
  • Levels 1-2 (Monastic, Royal): Learn to recognize, not use actively
  • Level 3 (Obsolete): Helpful for reading old texts, avoid in speech
  • Levels 4-5 (Literary, Technical): Use in appropriate professional/artistic contexts
  • Level 6 (Formal): Essential for business and hierarchical settings
  • Level 8 (Casual): Wait for Thais to initiate before using
  • Level 9 (Vulgar): Learn to recognize, never use as a foreigner
