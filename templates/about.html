🪷 About ThaiBridge AI เกี่ยวกับ

ThaiBridge AI is a web app for learning the Thai language through the lens of Thai culture and Theravada Buddhism. It teaches the Thai alphabet and vowels with Paiboon-style romanization, vocabulary by topic, grammar, lessons, and cultural and temple etiquette — supported by an AI tutor.

This is an honest learning project and portfolio piece, built largely with AI-assisted development. It runs locally and is still being improved — it is not a finished product.

Built by Joshua Kay · github.com/joshuablakemorekay
